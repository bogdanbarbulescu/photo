About Me

Hello, I'm Bogdan. About eight years ago, I fell in love with landscape photography.

It started as a hobby but quickly grew into a passion that brings me tranquility, sparks my curiosity, invites introspection, and fills me with joy and awe.

This journey has allowed me to connect with fantastic people, sharing perspectives and learning together. It's also challenged me to push my limits, venturing further afield to capture unique and special places. While the 'perfect light' isn't always present, I've learned that the experience itself is invaluable.

Through this portfolio, my goal is to share the images that mean the most to me and, hopefully, pass along a little of the wonder and emotion I experienced while creating them.
